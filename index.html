What Does 'Burnout' Look Like?


  • Exhaustion

What Is Burnout?


A prolonged response to chronic interpersonal stressors while on the job.


prolonged and chronic


"I feel like it's eroding my soul."

You Know the Feeling

How Does It Happen?


Six Stressors of Burnout


  • Workload
  • Control
  • Reward
  • Community
  • Fairness
  • Values

Positive or Negative Stress?


"If you love your work, you can easily fall victim to burnout."

Havard Business Review - "When Passion Leads to Burnout"


Purpose-driven work - work that people love and feel passionately about - can create an "obsessive passion".


Employees driven by purpose are significantly more stressed and score lower for well-being, resilience, and self-efficacy than those who are not

Why Should We Care?


  • Poor quality of work
  • Low morale
  • Absenteeism
  • Turnover
  • Health problems
  • Depression or anger
  • Family problems


WHO Internal Classification of Diseases - ICD-11 QD85

How Can We Help?


  • Sustainable (and interesting) workload
  • Choice and control
  • Recognition and rewards (not just money)
  • Supportive work communtiy
  • Fairness, respect, social justice
  • Clear values - Meaningful work

And There's More!


Prevention is better than treatment.


Engagement is the key - pay attention, talk about it.


Build it, Monitor it, Improve it.
